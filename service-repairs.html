Service and Repairs

Service and Repairs Overview

We would like you to get the most from your car, and that means getting your service done fast and to the hightest of standards to keep your car roadworthy at a minimum.

We offer service packages to make sure your vehicle gets regular maintenance it needs. Our centres are fitted with all the latest diagnostic and repair equipment so that we can identify faults quickly and accurately. This means we complete remedial work quickly and return your car to you as soon as possible. Contact us straight away to discuss a plan that best suits your needs.

Service

Interim Service
  • Change oil & oil filter
  • Check and report all lights & bulbs, brake lights
  • Check and report on exhaust, battery & wipers
  • Check and report on tyre thread depths
  • Check and report on front and rear brake pads and discs
  • Check and report on brake, clutch, power steering & washer bottle fluid levels
Full Service
  • Check and report all lights & bulbs, brake lights
  • Check and report on exhaust, battery & wipers
  • Check and report on tyre thread depths
  • Check and report on front and rear brake pads and discs
  • Check and report on brake, clutch, power steering & washer bottle fluid levels
  • Check and report on the suspension which includes shocks, struts, springs, bushes and links

Repairs

We can look after all aspects of your vehicle from large to small, inside and out including: Brake, windscreen wipers, exhausts, timing belts, light bulbs, batteries, alloy refurbishment and much more!

We have access to the best mechanics to resolve your motoring issues. We only use the best of equipment and materials.
